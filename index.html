My Trip to Colorado

To ring in the new year of 2018, a small group of friends and I decided to make a trip to Colorado. First time visiting The Cenntennial State for all but one of us, so we were all pretty pumped for the adventure.


Picture of the group!




The trip started bright and early on Friday, December 30th, at 4 A.M. to be exact.

That's Bekah, my bff, and me about to kick off the roadtrip.

After about 10 or 11 hours of driving on what seemed like the never ending I-70 across Kansas we saw a sign welcoming us to Colorado! Special thank you to my pals on the

My Favorite Murder

for entertainment along the way. Have some extra time on your hands? Check 'em out! So we had two and a half days to see what Colorado had to offer. Not much but hey that's vacationing on an extreme budget and poor planning.



Day One!

  • Visit some shops and stock up on goodies exclusive to Colorado and other select states!
  • Get the one fancy dinner that our budget allowed.
  • Check out as many breweries as we had time for.
  • Get to bed early, we had a full day planned the next day.

Day Two!

  • Quickie breakfast of donuts and coffee
  • Visit Rocky Mountain National Park and soak in the scenery
  • See My Morning Jacket and Tune-Yards in concert! (This was my favorite part)
  • Bar hopping? Dont worry our nice Uber driver got us home safely.

Day Three!

  • Make a fancy brunch with bloody marys in hopes to cure our hangovers
  • Naps
  • Pizza and more breweries
  • Bar hopping and dancing to ring in the new year.
  • Started off 2018 at a diner where the waitress' uniforms included sequin leggings.(questionable decision IMO.)
  • Thanks again to the random Uber driver who shuttled us home.
